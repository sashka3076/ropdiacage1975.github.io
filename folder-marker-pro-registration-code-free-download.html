      • Under Programs click the Uninstall a Program link.
      • Select 'Folder Marker' and right click, then select Uninstall/Change.
      • Click 'Yes' to confirm the uninstallation.

      How do I uninstall Folder Marker in Windows XP?

      • Click 'Start'
      • Click on 'Control Panel'
      • Click the Add or Remove Programs icon.
      • Click on 'Folder Marker', then click 'Remove/Uninstall.'
      • Click 'Yes' to confirm the uninstallation.
